Creating Real Value

Sam’s Holdings is a sister company to Sam’s Xpress Car Wash and Sam’s Mart Convenience stores. We select sites and develop high-quality Commercial properties not only for our internal clients, but also for third party users. Furthermore, as current owners of many well-located parcels of land, many of which are located at signalized intersections, we seek to repurpose these sites as attractive, neighborhood friendly properties. We understand how to build and manage well-located projects that foster growth and impact our communities.

For more than 26 years, we have focused on building next generation gasoline stations and carwash facilities that provide superior results. Experienced in everything from C-stores to retail and strip centers, our team can handle all aspects from site selection and planning to construction management. We understand that our properties leave an impression on our development partners, and we pride ourselves on providing superior properties by doing things right. Our thorough development process includes:

  • MSA & submarket research
  • Demographic & psychographic analysis
  • Identification
  • Contracting
  • Acquisitions
  • Due diligence, entitlements, permitting
  • Design development
  • Construction management
